Recovery Workout

Recovery workouts are essential for maintaing fitness while allowing your body to heal after long or hard runs. This recovery workout will be sure to leave you feeling refreshed and loose:

  • 10 min jog (40% effort)
  • 20 min stretches (stretching exercises)
  • 30 min continous run (50% effort)
  • 10 min stretches
  • 10 min core strengthening (core exercises)
