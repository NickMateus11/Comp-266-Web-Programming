Weight-Loss Workout

Weight-Loss workouts are great for burning extra fat and leaning up your core, legs, and arms. This weight-loss workout will help you burn calories and stay healthy:

  • 5 min warm-up walk
  • 5 min stretches (stretching exercises)
  • Repeat 5-8 times:
    • 4 min power walk
    • 1-2 min jog
  • 10 min lunges (lunge excerises)
