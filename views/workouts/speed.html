Speed Workout

Speed workouts are important for building strength and endurance in both your legs and lungs. This speed workout will test your fitness:

  • 5 min warm-up run
  • 5 min stretches (stretching exercises)
  • 4x200m runs (80% effort)
  • 2x400m runs (80% effort)
  • 8x100m runs (90% effort)
  • Explosive quad/hamstring excerises (squat excerises)
