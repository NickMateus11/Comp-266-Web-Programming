Interval Workout

Interval workouts are essential for developing fitness without the risk of overtraining or injury. This interval workout will help build your fitness, strength, and cardio all in one:

  • 10 min jog (50% effort)
  • 5 min stretches (stretching exercises)
  • 30 min run:
    • 5 min - 60% effort
    • 2 min - 80% effort
    • 5 min - 60% effort
    • 2 min - 85% effort
    • 5 min - 60% effort
    • 1 min - 90% effort
    • 5 min - 40% effort
    • 5 min - 60% effort
  • 10 min dynamic stretching (dynamic stretching exercises)
